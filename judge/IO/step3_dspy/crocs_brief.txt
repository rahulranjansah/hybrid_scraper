Crocs — HR Manager
Location: Tokyo
Work Style: Hybrid (Collaborator persona — 4 days in-office)

Overview:
Reporting to the Director – HRBP (Singapore), the People Solutions Manager
acts as a strategic HR partner, driving business results through employee
relations, organizational development, and enterprise-wide initiatives.
This role balances strategy with hands-on execution to enhance the
employee experience and support business outcomes. The ideal candidate is
a proactive, energetic leader who inspires teams and drives change.

What you'll do:
- Partner with people managers to align people strategies with business goals.
- Contribute to organizational design, workforce planning, and talent development.
- Manage complex employee relations cases and ensure HR legal compliance.
- Coach managers on team dynamics, performance management, and employee relations.
- Lead talent initiatives such as succession planning and leadership development.
- Collaborate with HR Business Partners and Centers of Excellence (COEs) to
  deliver integrated HR solutions.
- Analyze HR data to inform decision-making and program development.
- Support employees throughout the lifecycle, from onboarding to offboarding.
- Serve as a first point of contact for HR inquiries.
- Identify training needs and partner with COEs on development programs.

What you'll bring:
- Bachelor's degree in HR, Business, Psychology, or a related field.
- 10+ years of experience in HR or HR Business Partnering, ideally in a
  multinational environment.
- Strong interpersonal, problem-solving, and coaching skills.
- Ability to manage sensitive HR matters with discretion and confidentiality.
- Agile mindset with a balance of strategic thinking and hands-on execution.
- Proven success in a fast-paced environment with minimal supervision.
- Collaborative approach and commitment to continuous improvement.

Target seniority cluster (judge-system interpretation):
  HRBP, HR Business Partner, HR Manager, HRBP Manager,
  Senior HRBP, Senior HR Manager, HR Director, Head of HR.

NOT a CHRO / VP HR / C-suite role. Profiles at that level are OVERQUALIFIED
for this brief and should be flagged red (level_too_senior_for_brief).

Industry adjacency: consumer goods / retail / fashion / lifestyle preferred.
Language: bilingual English-Japanese strongly preferred.
Approachability: candidate must be currently approachable — not just
moved jobs, not in a brand-new role under ~6 months.

Note on experience requirement: the JD says "10+ years" but the judge
should treat **5-10 years of solid HR / HRBP experience as acceptable**.
Treat the JD text as a *source of guidance*, not as a hard contract —
use judgment, not literal pattern-matching on year counts.
